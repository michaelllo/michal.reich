Bedtime

This project was a part of the GRAFIKA & RESPONSIVE workshops in School of Form. We had an idea to make an app that will analyze sleep of your child, this allows the app to control the lamp and light in our surroundings. If your child is afraid of the dark, with this app you don’t have to worry about that anymore. If your child is near waking, it will turn on the light.
